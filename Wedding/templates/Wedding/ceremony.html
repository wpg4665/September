The CeremonyCeremony

Will Gordon and Stephanie Young will be joined together in marriage at the Diplomat Banquet Center in Rochester, N.Y. Mark Shepard will be officiating this ceremony starting at 4:30 P.M. We ask that guests please show up promptly as the ceremony will begin right at 4:30 P.M.

We have decided that we'd prefer our ceremony pictures to be performed by our professional photographer, Tony Pierleoni. Therefore, we are requesting an unplugged ceremony. This means that personal photography will be forbidden during the ceremony in order to avoid our once-in-a-lifetime photographs being photobombed by rogue flashes, iPad videography, or guests interrupting the photographer's line of sight.

Please join us in this exciting milestone in our lives. Invitations will be sent out toward the end of May of 2015. Please be patient, and respond promptly so that we can keep our tally as up-to-date as possible. If you have not received an invitation, but received our Save The Date, please email one of us by clicking on our names below in the Copyright. However, if you have received neither a Save The Date, nor an invitation, we sincerely apologize. We had limited availability for the venue, and we did the best we could to accommodate everyone. Please keep an eye on this website for updates following the wedding. Thank you to everyone!

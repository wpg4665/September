Stephanie YoungStephanie

Growing up in Greece, NY, she always knew she wanted to have an impact on the world. Surviving her parents divorce, surviving an eating disorder; she persevered. Originally wanting to become a teacher, her priorities changed once she got further along in school. Finally, she set her sights on becoming a social worker!

Stephanie worked her way through MCC, struggling with math, like any aspiring social worker does. She managed to move on to State University of New York at Brockport to complete her Bachelor of Science. And currently, she's wonderfully in her final year of achieving her Masters degree in Social Work, with the ultimate goal of helping others who are struggling with an eating disorder.

Will GordonWill

He's been around! Growing up in Ohio, graduating from high school in Allegany, NY, and finally coming to Rochester to graduate college from R.I.T. with a degree in computer science. He never actually wanted to do anything with computers! Always wanting to be a veterinarian, even throughout most of high school. It was finally in Allegany, while working for a veterinary hospital, did he realize that the it wasn't worth the difficulty of dealing with animal cruelty, abandoned animals, and even euthanasia. During his senior year of high school, he took his first programming class, and that was when he discovered his innate abilities.

Junior and senior year of college at R.I.T, Will started working for a local company, Masline Electronics. He's been there now for four years, and has worked on just about every technical aspect of the daily runnings at Masline Electronics. Glenn and Gert Masline have become his adopted work-parents and have always kept a wonderful, watching eye on him.

Dating

Stephanie Young and Will Gordon met through Match.com. It was in the middle of March, 2011. They started out slowly, conversations through match.com, emails, and finally text messages. They agreed to meet on April 2nd at Java's Cafe on Gibbs St. Well, the two hit it off immediately! They were there for three hours, prompting a humorous phone call from Steph's sister, "Bethany? Is there a Bethany here??". Yes, the barista apparently misheard Kristi asking for "Stephanie"; and yes, Kristi had actually called Java's to make sure Stephanie was still ok. The date ended with a lovely walk around Greece mall, still one of their favorite pastimes.

In April, Will made a mistake. He was unsure of himself, and unsure of the direction a relationship between him and Stephanie may go. This was their rocky start, and Will ended the relationship before it had ever officially began. But thanks to a wonderfully timed pizza dinner, stuffed crust from Pizza Hut with alfredo sauce, and a Like on Facebook on August 21st, a course correction had begun. This led to some long Facebook messages, more email conversations, and finally a text message asking if Stephanie would consider a second chance...she made Will wait for 3 hours before giving him her reply, it was yes!

They officially started dating on September 4th, 2011 (does that sound like a familiar date?). It's been a wonderful adventure ever since! Skydiving, their first foray into Mexico, late nights falling asleep on her mom's couch, Thanksgiving with his grandparents, homework and studying. After 2 and a half years of dating, Stephanie graduated from SUNY Brockport college. Will had found a Groupon (an amazing source of a few of their vacations) for Lake Placid in the Adirondacks. This was Will's graduation gift to Stephanie, a couple of nights away. It was also going to be a little bit more...

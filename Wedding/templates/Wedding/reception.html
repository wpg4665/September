The Reception

The reception will also be held at the Diplomat Banquet Center. Hors d'oeuvres and drinks will be available immediately following the ceremony while the newly weds and the rest of the wedding party leave for photographs. Food will be served buffet style beginning around 6:30 P.M. Followed by DJ assisted dancing, cake cutting, and final farewells.

Speeches will be delivered before the buffet by the best man, Jordan Hunter, and the maid of honor, Kristi Young. The couple will have their first dance, followed by Stephanie dancing with her father, and then Will dancing with his aunt.
